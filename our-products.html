PERILAV

Safe, Simple, Effective

The PERILAV system is a safe, simple and effective portable pulsatile irrigation for:
  • Removal of necrotic tissue, exudate and debris
  • Removal and/or reduction of bioburden/biofilm

The system can be used effectively by any properly trained clinician to clean a wound with incredible efficiency and effectiveness like no other system on the market and we have the case studies to prove it.

View our case studies

How it works

The Perilav pulsed irrigator generates an 8-12 PSI which is within the safe irrigation pressure range for wounds, yet high enough to debride necrotic tissue and disrupt biofilm

Clinicians can choose almost any type of solution for irrigation from saline, sterile water, to any number of wound cleansers.

Our innovative enclosure bags are sterile, completely cover the wound site, capture fluid and debris, prevent exposure to any aerosolized particles and make clean up easy.

Our enclosure bags come in two designs, one for torso wounds and one for extremity wounds and are available with different window sizes and diameters to accommodate any situation.

Everything you need to expertly clean and debride your patients’ wounds come in the Perilav Jet Lavage kits.
